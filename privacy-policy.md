# Wallora Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** July 2026

## Introduction

Wallora respects your privacy.

This Privacy Policy explains what information may be collected while using the application and how that information is used.

## Information Collection

Wallora does not require users to create an account.

The application does not collect personal information such as:

- Name
- Address
- Phone number
- Email address

unless you voluntarily contact the developer.

## Advertising

Wallora uses Google AdMob to display advertisements.

Google and its partners may collect certain information to provide personalized or non-personalized ads in accordance with their own privacy policies.

More information:

https://policies.google.com/privacy

https://support.google.com/admob

## Wallpapers

Wallora allows users to download wallpapers to their own device.

Downloaded wallpapers are stored only on the user's device.

Wallora does not upload downloaded wallpapers to any external server.

## Permissions

Depending on the Android version, Wallora may request permissions required for:

- Saving wallpapers
- Setting wallpapers
- Accessing media files

Permissions are used only for application functionality.
